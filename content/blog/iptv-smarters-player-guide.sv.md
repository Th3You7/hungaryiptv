# IPTV Smarters Player Guide: IPTV Smart Setup and M3U IPTV Explained

## What Is IPTV and IPTV TV?

**IPTV** (Internet Protocol Television) is a modern way to watch television using the internet. Instead of cable or satellite, **IPTV TV** streams channels, movies, and series online using apps and IPTV players.

Today, millions of users prefer **IPTV TV** because it is cheaper, faster, and supports HD, Full HD, and 4K streaming.

---

## What Is IPTV Smarters Player?

**IPTV Smarters Player** is one of the most popular IPTV applications used to watch IPTV on Smart TVs, Android devices, iPhones, and Windows.  
It supports **M3U IPTV playlists** and Xtream Codes API login.

With **IPTV Smarters Player**, you can watch:

- Live TV channels  
- Movies and series (VOD)  
- Catch-up TV  
- Multi-screen IPTV TV  

---

## What Is IPTV Smart?

**IPTV Smart** refers to Smart IPTV apps and smart devices that support IPTV streaming.  
Examples include:

- Smart IPTV apps on Smart TVs  
- IPTV Smarters Player  
- IBO Player Pro  
- Smarter Pro  

Using **IPTV Smart apps**, you can watch IPTV TV on Samsung, LG, Android TV, Apple TV, and more.
