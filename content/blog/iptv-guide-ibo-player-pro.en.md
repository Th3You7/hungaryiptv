# IPTV TV Guide: How to Configure IPTV on IBO Player Pro (Step-by-Step)

## What Is IPTV TV?

**IPTV TV** (Internet Protocol Television) is a modern way to watch television through the internet instead of traditional cable or satellite. With IPTV, you can access thousands of live TV channels, movies, and series on Smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, and PCs.

IPTV TV is becoming very popular because it is affordable, flexible, and supports HD, Full HD, and 4K streaming quality.

---

## What Is Smart IPTV and IPTV M3U?

**Smart IPTV** is a popular IPTV player application used on Smart TVs to play IPTV playlists.

**IPTV M3U** is a playlist format that contains all TV channels and video-on-demand content. Most IPTV providers use M3U links or Xtream Codes to deliver IPTV services.

With a reliable IPTV provider and stable internet connection, you can enjoy smooth streaming without buffering.

## Step 1: Install IBO Player Pro

1. Open the App Store or Google Play Store on your device  
2. Search for **IBO Player Pro**  
3. Download and open the app  

---

## Step 2: Get Your MAC Address and Device Key

When you open IBO Player Pro, you will see:

- MAC Address  
- Device Key  

Save these details because you will need them to upload your IPTV playlist.

---

## Step 3: Upload Your IPTV M3U Playlist

1. Visit the official IBO Player Pro website  
2. Enter your **MAC Address** and **Device Key**  
3. Upload your **IPTV M3U link** or enter Xtream Codes API details  
4. Save the configuration  

Restart the app on your TV.

---

## Step 4: Start Watching IPTV TV

After restarting, your IPTV TV channels, movies, and series will load automatically.  
You can now enjoy high-quality streaming with **strong and stable IPTV servers**.
